Travel Destination Guide

Vijayawada

Vijayawada, formerly known as Bezawada, is the second largest city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located on the banks of the Krishna River surrounded by the hills of the Eastern Ghats, known as the Indrakeeladri Hills.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Telangana. The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, the landmark is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated into the emblem of Telangana.

Visakhapatnam (Vizag)

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, Viśākha, or Waltair, is the largest and most populous metropolitan city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located between the Eastern Ghats and the coast of the Bay of Bengal.

Tirupati

Tirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is known for the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the holiest Hindu pilgrimage sites dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Warangal

Warangal is a city in the Indian state of Telangana. It is known for its rich history, including the Warangal Fort, Thousand Pillar Temple, and the beautiful Bhadrakali Temple.

Kurnool

Kurnool is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its historical significance and landmarks such as the Konda Reddy Fort, Orvakal Rock Gardens, and Belum Caves.

Guntur

Guntur is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its rich culture, historical sites, and delicious cuisine, especially spicy Andhra dishes.

Karimnagar

Karimnagar is a city in the Indian state of Telangana. It is famous for its historical monuments, such as the Elgandal Fort, and is known for its traditional festivals and handicrafts.

Ongole

Ongole is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is renowned for its cattle market, Ongole cattle breed, and is also known for its granite industry.
